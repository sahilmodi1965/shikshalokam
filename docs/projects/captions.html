How this page works

Brainstorm → variants → approve.

Per session

  1. Sonal: "draft Tuesday's research insight post about [topic]"
  2. Claude drops 2–3 variants under Tuesday — Tech / Research Insight, each with platform tag (LinkedIn / X) and status: draft.
  3. Sonal copies the title of her preferred variant back into chat → status: approved.
  4. Once posted, Sonal flags "posted" → status: posted, archives at end of week to projects/captions/posted/.
  5. Pushback on a variant → 2–3 fresh variants next turn; structural correction routes to learnings/.

Caption voice — canonical rules
  • Opener: rhetorical question · one-line aphorism · pulled quote (attributed)
  • Cadence: stacked short lines + comma-dense context blocks
  • Pronoun register: on-the-team "we"
  • Emoji scaffolding: ▶️ YouTube link · 📍 context · 🔴 live · 👇 swipe/scroll
  • Hashtag stack: #InvokED2026 #MissionToMovement #CollectiveAction
  • Partner tagging: @Org with handle
  • Recurring vocabulary: collective action · mission to movement · har kadam shiksha ki oar..!
  • CTA closer: "Share your thoughts in the comments below 👇"

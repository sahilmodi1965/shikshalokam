What last year's convening produced — gives 6.0 invites pull
  • Scale of the room: 5.0 drew 1,000+ changemakers across Samaaj, Sarkaar, Bazaar and Sanchar.
  • The movement, by the numbers: ~1.2 million micro-improvements; ~343,000 leaders driving improvement.
  • Launched at 5.0: the Shikshagraha Commons; the Shikshagraha STEM Collective; the Shikshagraha Awards 2026 + coffee-table book "Small Steps to Build Great Schools."
  • Bright spots: Tamil Nadu's House System now in 37,500 schools; Tirunelveli enrolment 75% → 95.8%; Nagaland NLNF 3.0 = 22,000 micro-improvement programs.

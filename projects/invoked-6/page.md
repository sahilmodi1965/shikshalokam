# InvokED 6.0 — invitation workspace

The live workspace for **InvokED 6.0** invitations. Everything ShikshaLokam learned writing the
InvokED 5.0 ("Mission to Movement", 6–7 Feb 2026) invites is pre-loaded here — the template skeleton,
the audience→signatory map, the standing phrases, and a momentum bank of verified 5.0 outcomes to
weave in. When Sonal opens Claude Code and says *"draft the speaker invite for [Name]"* or *"the
government-official invite,"* the brain already holds the form.

## How this page works

- Sonal in chat: *"InvokED 6.0 — draft the [segment] invite for [Name]"* → Claude pulls the matching
  template below, fills the 6.0 specifics, adds a personalised recall paragraph if it's a VIP, and
  adds a `### Invite — <segment / name>` section with `status: draft`.
- Sonal pushes detail / says "use this" → `status: approved`. Once sent → `status: sent`, logged.
- Corrections to wording route to a learning, never a silent rewrite (CLAUDE.md rule 8).

Statuses: `template` (skeleton, awaiting 6.0 specifics) → `draft` (a specific invite written) →
`approved` → `sent`.

## Momentum bank — verified 5.0 lines to weave in

Use these to show what last year's convening produced (gives 6.0 invites pull). All cited to
[[invoked-5-proceedings-2026]].

- **Scale of the room:** 5.0 drew an audience of 1,000+ changemakers across Samaaj, Sarkaar, Bazaar
  and Sanchar.
- **The movement, by the numbers (event morning):** ~1.2 million micro-improvements; ~343,000 leaders.
- **Launched at 5.0:** the Shikshagraha Commons (open, multilingual practice repository) and the
  Shikshagraha STEM Collective; the Shikshagraha Awards 2026 + the coffee-table book *"Small Steps to
  Build Great Schools."*
- **Bright spots:** Tamil Nadu's House System now in 37,500 schools; Tirunelveli enrolment 75% → 95.8%;
  Nagaland NLNF 3.0 = 22,000 micro-improvement programs.
- **Quote bank (verbatim):** Wendy Kopp — *"Leadership in the new age… is about listening deeply,
  sharing responsibility, and enabling collective wisdom."* · Sanjay Purohit — *"Do one small thing…
  then you're already a part of Shikshagraha."* · Cynthia McCaffrey (UNICEF) — *"We don't want
  charity, we want a chance."* · Dr Rangan — *"Leaders make decisions, but ownership is about
  choices."*

> Standing phrases (verbatim-reusable): "our annual global dialogue on education leadership" · "both
> reflective and catalytic—connecting macro perspectives on system transformation with micro-stories
> of leadership and agency" · "Your presence would lend immense strength and direction to these
> dialogues." · "I look forward to welcoming you to InvokED 6.0."

# Captions — rolling option pool

Caption candidates grouped by the weekday rhythm Sonal authored in `routes/captions.md`. Each slot can carry 2–3 variants. Status moves `draft → approved → posted`. Posted captions archive weekly to `projects/captions/posted/<YYYY-MM-DD>.md`.

## How this page works

- Sonal in chat: *"draft Tuesday's research insight post about [topic]"* → Claude drops 2–3 variants under **Tuesday — Tech / Research Insight**, each with platform tag (`LinkedIn` / `X`) and `status: draft`.
- Sonal copies the title (first line) of the variant she wants back into chat → status flips to `approved`. Voice rule that landed is noted under the variant.
- Sonal flags posted → status flips to `posted`, archives at end of week.
- "Not my voice" / "rewrite" on any variant → 2–3 fresh variants in next turn, structural feedback routes to `learnings/`.

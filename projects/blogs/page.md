# Blogs — idea queue and WIP drafts

Two-layer workspace:

1. **Idea queue** — angles Sonal wants to develop. One `### Idea — <Title>` section per candidate. Status: `seed → brief → outline → drafting → published`. Page stays light.
2. **WIP drafts** — once an idea reaches `outline` or beyond, the full draft overflows to `projects/blogs/drafts/<slug>.md` and the section here keeps a one-paragraph synopsis + link.

## How this page works

- Sonal in chat: *"new blog idea — [topic]"* → Claude adds an `### Idea — <Title>` section with `status: seed`, captures the framing + open questions.
- Sonal pushes detail across sessions → status advances (`seed → brief → outline → drafting`). Full draft moves to `drafts/<slug>.md` once it's >300 words. Page section becomes a synopsis card.
- Once published, status flips to `published`, link to live URL on shikshalokam.org/blogs/, section shrinks to a one-line entry under **Published reference**.

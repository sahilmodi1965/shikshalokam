# Nagaland Coffee Book — stories in progress

A working pool of stories for ShikshaLokam's Nagaland coffee-table book. Stories begin as `wip`, move to `review-ready` when Sonal flags them, and `locked` once final. Each story is one `### Story — <Title>` section. The HTML mirror at `docs/projects/nagaland-coffee-book.html` carries the same shape with copy-friendly titles.

## Project intent

The Nagaland coffee book sits inside ShikshaLokam's broader Small Steps / Micro Miracles series — visual-first volumes that carry **stories of teachers, ed-leaders, communities, and students** who moved one specific outcome in one specific place. Distinct from the Brazil presentation (slide format) and the SLC blogs (long-form first-person) — coffee books carry **short, image-paired vignettes** (≤300 words per story).

Known anchor outcome for Nagaland: **DEP (District Education Programme) — 400 teachers, 200 schools, project-based learning take-up.** See [[shikshalokam-brazil-stories-may2026]] for the canonical macro framing.

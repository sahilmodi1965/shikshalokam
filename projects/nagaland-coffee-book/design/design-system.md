# Micro Miracles CTB · 3rd edition — design system

Design direction for the 3rd edition of the Micro Miracles coffee-table book, drawing on Nagaland
shawl, textile, and motif imagery gathered after NLNF 3.0. Sits inside [[page]] (the story pool).

## Palette — "Cool woven + Naga red" (locked 2026-06-25)

Chosen direction: **Option B (cool woven) leads, with Naga red carried through as a recurring accent.**

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|------|------|-----|-----|
| Dominant | Cobalt | `#1E3A8A` | covers, section bands, headers |
| Secondary | Azure | `#4A7FC9` | sawtooth bands, sub-accents |
| Ground | Ivory | `#EFE6D3` | page background, median bands |
| Ink | Charcoal | `#1A1A1A` | body text, diamond-lattice motifs |
| **Accent** | **Naga Red** | `#C6202B` | story-number tags, spear "small-step" markers, pull-quote rules, hornbill corner accent, one bold cover detail |

Principle: blue is the calm backbone for reading; **red is the recurring heartbeat** — present on
every spread, never dominant.

## Motif vocabulary (sampled from pattern 1–7)

- **Mithun-horn + sun rosette** (pic 3) — hero emblem → cover crest + section openers
- **Median band** (pics 6, 7) — full-width section dividers / chapter breaks
- **Spear-with-tassel** (pic 4) — "small step" progress marker beside each story (render in red)
- **Hourglass / double-spear row** (pics 1, 4) — running header & footer rule
- **Sawtooth triangle band** (pics 2, 5) — secondary divider, azure
- **Diamond / cowrie-dot lattice** (pics 2, 3, 5) — bullets, page-number frames
- **Hornbill** (pics 6, 7) — recurring corner accent (red)

## Motif → theme mapping

- **Micro-improvement** → the small repeated unit (spear, diamond, hourglass) marching across a
  band = "many small steps, again and again, across the year."
- **Collective effort** → the median band where figures stand together; the banding that holds the
  whole cloth as one.

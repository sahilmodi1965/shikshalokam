## What the brain knows about us

**Mission** *(from `wiki/concepts/mission.md`)*

ShikshaLokam exists to enable a people's movement — Shikshagraha — to strengthen India's 1 million public schools. We are co-builders of DIKSHA (national education platform with Government of India). Our work helps the system see better, solve better, and sustain change better.

**Our impact, in our own words** *(from `wiki/sources/shikshagraha-narrative-2026.md` — "As ShikshaLokam" section)*

- **ACCESS:** 8 lakhs+ micro-improvements led by school leaders and teachers via DIKSHA
- **NARRATIVE:** building the story of 'improving public schools'
- **IMPACT:** micro-improvement approach institutionalised at federal level + across Bihar (30K middle schools, 60L children), Punjab (improvement since 2021), Haryana, Nagaland, Andhra Pradesh
- **Ecosystem:** built network of 50+ civil society organisations since 2017; won Schwab Foundation award for collective social innovation at WEF 2023 (via Punjab education collective)
- **Scale:** 27 states & UTs, 570000+ leaders, 800000+ MIPs

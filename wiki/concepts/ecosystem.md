# Ecosystem

Where ShikshaLokam sits. Partners, funders, and the wider field.

## Founder / patron

- **Shibulal Family Philanthropic Initiatives (SFPI)** — ShikshaLokam founded under SFPI in 2017.
- **SD Shibulal** — Co-founder, Infosys; former CEO. Co-founder, SFPI. Founder, ShikshaLokam.
- **Kumari Shibulal** — Founder & Chairperson, SFPI.

Source: [shikshalokam.org](https://shikshalokam.org/)

## Government

- **Ministry of Education, Government of India** — partner for scale.
- **Department of School Education and Literacy** + **Department of Higher Education** — engaged at InvokED 2026.
- **State governments** — 10 states cited (HUNDRED, 2024); specific names not surfaced in fetched material.

Sources: [thewire.in](https://m.thewire.in/article/ptiprnews/invoked-2026-brings-global-and-grassroots-leaders-together-as-shikshagraha-announces-2026-awardees), [hundred.org](https://hundred.org/en/innovations/shikshalokam)

## Civil-society partners (named in public material)

- **Mantra4Change** — co-organiser of InvokED 5.0 (2026) alongside Shikshagraha and ShikshaLokam.
  Note: per INTENT principle 7, this brain does not transplant Mantra4Change content. Cross-mentions are sourced.

- **Childaid Network Foundation** — Martin Kasper (Founder / President) attended InvokED 2026.

## Tech / open-source ecosystem

- **Sunbird (EkStep Foundation)** — open-source building blocks; ShikshaLokam built ELEVATE on top and contributes back. Sunbird is the same stack underlying DIKSHA.
- **DIKSHA** — national education infrastructure (Ministry of Education / NCERT / CIET); hosts ShikshaLokam capabilities.

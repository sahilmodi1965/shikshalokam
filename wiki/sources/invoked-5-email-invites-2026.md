# InvokED 5.0 Email Invitation Library

Faithful extraction of the InvokED 5.0 invitation library (~35 invite blocks → ~17 reusable
archetypes + a long tail of personalised VIP variants). This is the **direct model for InvokED 6.0
invitations** — the 6.0-ready, placeholdered versions live in [[../../projects/invoked-6/page]].
Templates here are reproduced as in source (merge fields shown as `<<First Name>>` etc.).

## The common skeleton (every invite)

1. **Salutation** — "Dear `<<First Name>>`," (govt adds "Ji"; "Dear Dr./Ms./Mr."; mentors "…Sir").
2. **Pleasantry** — "I hope this message finds you well [and in great spirits]." / "Hope you are
   doing well." / "Trust this message finds you well."
3. **Hook / framing** — "It gives me great pleasure to reach out as we prepare for the fifth edition
   of **InvokED**, our annual global dialogue on education leadership." + legacy line.
4. **Personalised recall paragraph** (VIPs only) — a specific memory of where they met / their past
   InvokED contribution / their work.
5. **The ask** — invite as speaker / partner / juror / closed-door participant / guest.
6. **What it is / WIIFT** — "This milestone edition marks five years… uniting Samaaj, Sarkaar,
   Bazaar, and Sanchaar…" + segment-specific bullets where used.
7. **Logistics** — "scheduled for **February 6 and 7, 2026** at the Prestige Centre for Performing
   Arts (PCPA), Bengaluru." (Broad invites use a Date / Time / Venue block.)
8. **Soft CTA** — "Please do save the dates, and my team would be happy to connect…" (broad: "Save
   the dates, and register here." dinner: "reply… and accept the calendar invite.")
9. **Forward-looking close** — "I look forward to welcoming you to InvokED 5.0."
10. **Sign-off + signature** — "Warm regards," + name + title.

## Standing phrases (reusable verbatim)

- "our annual global dialogue on education leadership" (standing descriptor of InvokED)
- "This milestone edition marks five years of InvokED as a platform uniting Samaaj, Sarkaar, Bazaar,
  and Sanchaar to drive systemic transformation in public education."
- "InvokED 5.0 will be both reflective and catalytic—connecting macro perspectives on system
  transformation with micro-stories of leadership and agency."
- "will bring together leaders, educators, and changemakers from across the country [and the world]
  to share ideas, celebrate progress, and explore how we can strengthen the movement for quality
  education."
- "Your presence would lend immense strength and direction to these dialogues." *(most-repeated line)*
- "Please do save the dates, and my team would be happy to connect to share more details about your
  participation."
- "I look forward to welcoming you to InvokED 5.0."

## What varies across personalised VIP invites

Only (1) salutation form, (2) the personalised recall paragraph (where they met / their work / past
InvokED contribution), and sometimes (3) a session-specific ask block (named slot, date, time).
Returning guests get a "Your keynote at InvokED 4.0…" hook; some carry a New-Year greeting.
**CC convention** on VIP outreach: Neeraj, Khushboo, Santosh.

## Logistics stated (5.0)

- Main event: **6–7 February 2026**, 9 AM onwards, **Prestige Centre for Performing Arts (PCPA),
  Bengaluru**. (Govt/print templates drop "Prestige.")
- Welcome Dinner: **5 Feb 2026** (SFPI). Learning Journey: **3–5 Feb 2026**. Strategic Partners
  Roundtable: **7 Feb, 12:00–1:30 PM**. Day 2 = **Shikshagraha Utsav**.
- RSVP: VIP = soft ("save the dates," team follows up, no form). Broad ecosystem/tech = Google Form
  register link. Dinner = reply + accept calendar invite.
- Standing links: InvokED `shikshalokam.org/invoked` · ShikshaLokam `shikshalokam.org` · SFPI
  `sfpi.org` · Shikshagraha `shikshagraha.org` · Awards `shikshagraha.org/awards/`.
- Recurring programme features to reference: **Brain Dates, Experience Zones, Dialogue Corner,
  Shikshagraha Awards, Shikshagraha Museum, Leadership Spotlights, Closed-Door / Closed-Group
  sessions, Strategic Partners Roundtable, Tech Track, Shikshagraha Utsav (Day 2).**

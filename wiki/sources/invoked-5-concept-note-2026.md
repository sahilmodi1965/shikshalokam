## What InvokED 5.0 was, and why

- The **fifth edition** of [InvokED](https://shikshalokam.org/invoked/) — ShikshaLokam's annual
  global dialogue on education leadership. It marks **five years** of convening leaders,
  practitioners, policymakers, and changemakers around education leadership and collective action.
- Framing: more than half of India's school-going children are still denied equitable access to
  quality education. Public schools are "the backbone of our national and global futures." The
  scale of inequity "demands collaboration rooted in trust, collective leadership, and community
  participation." InvokED has "evolved from a dialogue into a movement-building space."
- Animating questions (verbatim spirit): *What does collective action look like in practice? How
  do leaders — across Samaaj, Sarkaar, Bazaar, and Sanchar — create coherence, not silos? How can
  India's education leadership story inspire the Global South? How do we create public demand for
  urgent action to improve the quality of education in public schools — in India and beyond?*

## What the milestone edition focused on

InvokED 5.0 was designed to be **"both reflective and catalytic"**, connecting macro perspectives
on leadership and system transformation with micro-level stories of action and agency. Stated aims:

- Celebrate leadership and collective ownership shaping education transformation in India and beyond.
- Deepen collaboration among co-creators — government, civil society, and philanthropic partners.
- Anchor the next phase of Shikshagraha's movement, particularly the **250 Districts Mission**.
- Inspire urgency and agency — turning collective intent into coordinated action.

Bringing together diverse actors from **Samaaj, Sarkaar, Bazaar, and Sanchar** to co-create
pathways for stronger public education in India — and to imagine how those lessons shape a global
education movement "for one billion children and youth across Asia, Africa, and Latin America."

## Audience (1,000+ changemakers expected)

## "What can you gain?" — value by segment (verbatim spirit)

- **Government official:** showcase work and perspectives on stronger ecosystems; find collaboration
  partners across geographies; co-create InvokED as a platform elevating public schools as national assets.
- **Education CSO:** national stage for your theory of change; a platform for your stakeholders to
  share contributions and innovations; shape and join a national + global education movement.
- **Philanthropy organisation:** present your approach to equity; discover collaborations across India
  and globally; be a founding partner in shaping One Billion Futures.
- **Media / storytellers:** first-of-its-kind platform spotlighting leadership in public education;
  shape the public narrative around equity; amplify grassroots voices of students, teachers, leaders.
- **Academia:** present cutting-edge research on education equity; feed evidence into national and
  global movement-building.
- **Youth & communities:** share lived experiences and grassroots innovations; influence the design of
  stronger, inclusive public schools; connect with global peers working for equity.

# The Future is Collective (2025)

**Full citation:** Schwab Foundation for Social Entrepreneurship (2025). *The Future
is Collective: Advancing Collective Social Innovation to Address Society's Biggest
Challenges.* World Economic Forum.

**Trust tier:** Tier 1 — a report from the Schwab Foundation for Social
Entrepreneurship, the World Economic Forum's social-innovation foundation.

**URL:** https://www.weforum.org/stories/2025/04/collective-social-innovation/ ·
companion piece https://www.weforum.org/stories/2025/09/the-future-of-innovation-is-collective-but-only-if-we-reimagine-how-we-work-together/
**Verified:** 2026-05-21 via the `shikshalokam-research` skill (WebSearch — confirmed
the report title, the Schwab Foundation authorship, and that it names Shikshagraha).

## What it says (cite verbatim where it names us)

- The report shows how **collective social innovators** build systems that are more
  equitable and sustainable across generations.
- It highlights case studies where funders enabled platforms and networks — naming
  **MapBiomas** and **Shikshagraha** — bringing actors together across sectors,
  *"from global climate coalitions to grassroots education movements."*
- It names **Shibulal Family Philanthropic Initiatives (SFPI)** as enabling
  collective action and network-building in India *"by weaving Shikshagraha."*
- It carries the **Punjab Education Collective** story: four civil-society
  organisations plus government institutions helped Punjab rise from **13th to 1st**
  in India's national school rankings within two years, holding the position for
  five years.

## SL-messaging angle

**The power of collective action — external, third-party validation.** This is the
strongest available *outside* recognition that names Shikshagraha by name. Use it
whenever the messaging needs an authoritative voice to say collective action is the
operating logic for hard social problems — and that Shikshagraha is one of the
systems building it.

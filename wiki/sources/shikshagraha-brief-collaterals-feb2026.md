## Brochure structure (canonical content outline)

7-section structure for any Shikshagraha pitch collateral:

- **Open with question:** *"How do we work together to achieve education equity in our lifetime?"* or *"What is the education leadership needed to an effective public education system in India?"*
- **A. Challenge / Problem** — zoomed-out (scale, dropouts, learning gaps, negative perception) + zoomed-in (top-down tunnel vision / unempowered leadership / fragmented efforts)
- **B. Vision** — verbatim ShikshaLokam vision statement
- **C. Roadmap for Exponential Change** — Local Problems Local Solutions / Leaders at Every Level / Leveraging Collective Strength
- **D. Implementation** — three collectives
- **E. Community** — partner logos
- **F. CTA** — Khushboo's email
- **G. Social handles** — Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, website

- Use plain backgrounds

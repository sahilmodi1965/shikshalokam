## What these docs are canonical for

Caption voice: opening moves (rhetorical question / one-line aphorism / attributed pulled quote), cadence (stacked short lines + comma-dense context blocks), pronoun register (on-the-team "we"), emoji-as-scaffolding (▶️ for YouTube link, 📍 for context, 🔴 for live, 👇 for swipe/scroll), hashtag stacks (#InvokED2026 #MissionToMovement #CollectiveAction), partner tagging convention (@Org with handle), names + honorifics, recurring caption vocabulary ("collective action," "mission to movement," "har kadam shiksha ki oar..!"), CTA closer ("Share your thoughts in the comments below 👇").

## Not canonical for

Long-form donor / institutional voice, InvokED keynote material, regional-language voice (the docs are English with transliterated brand vocabulary only).

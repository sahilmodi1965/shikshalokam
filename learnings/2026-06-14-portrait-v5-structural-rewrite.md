# Masthanaiah portrait v5 — what Aquib's third review changed

Aquib Rizwan (in the room for the 20 Apr interview) supplied a full **8-section scaffold** and asked
for a structural rewrite. v5 follows his arc: origins/struggle → people who shaped him → principles →
teacher years (1986–95) → rise through administration → Hyderabad reforms → working at scale → vision.

## Facts Aquib corrected/added (he is the in-room authority; overrides auto-transcript)

- **Origin:** remote village in **Venkatagiri Taluk** (now Tirupati district).
- **Family of six** — four sisters, one brother; he the only one to stay in education.
- **Open-air "street school"** under a tree; studied only in daylight (no home lighting).
- **Two near-dropouts:** Class 5 (primary teacher intervened with his father) and **after Class 8**
  (needed to support his parents). ST hostel after Class 8 — *"only the walls were added."*
- **Mentors, named:** **Virupattam Sankar Raju** (primary); **V. Subbaram Reddy** (7th–8th, still
  visited past ninety); a diploma-engineer relative who *"changed his life."*
- **Teacher years:** single room; Sarpanch/committee + villagers upgrade to high school; Mandal
  President asks him to revive a second school; sole teacher classes 6–10; *"not the best, not the
  worst."* ~100 students as teacher → far more from a higher post (resolves the v4 flag).
- **Admin timeline:** selected **1993**; Deputy Educational Officer Hyderabad ~9 yrs; DEO Medak then
  Nalgonda. Micro-level inspection (MI) added alongside 1996 counselling transfers + rationalisation.
- **Scale layer (new):** **five years at SIEMAT** (scale-through-principals); **Jawahar Bal Bhavan**
  (200+ centres, two annual programmes); SLDP/IIM-A/250 heads; SALT; PM SHRI.

## Open flags carried into the page (verify before print)

- **Railway towns:** body uses Aquib's *Guntur–Renigunta*; transcript + ST-hostel location read
  *Gudur*. Geography favours Gudur — confirm.
- **PM SHRI:** Aquib said *"highest"*; transcript says **second**-highest after UP. Body hedges to
  "among the highest… second only to UP."
- **Residential-schools society:** APSWREIS (Aquib) vs APRS (transcript) — left out of body pending name.
- **SIEMAT** five-year tenure — Aquib's recall, not in transcript extract.

**Why:** Portrait facts must be right and provenance must survive. The in-room interviewer outranks a
garbled auto-transcript, but where they diverge on a checkable proper noun, flag rather than silently pick.

**How to apply:** For any ed-leader portrait, treat the interviewer's review as authoritative on lived
fact; keep a visible verify list for place names, acronyms, and superlatives that the transcript
contradicts. Never drop a correction silently — record it here and in the page's voice notes.

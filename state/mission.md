# Mission

## Self-description (verbatim)

- **Mission:** "Enable and amplify K-12 Education System Leadership in India."
- **Vision:** "Enabling enriching learning experiences for all children in India."
- **Self-framing:** "Education Leadership Catalyst," founded 2017 under the Shibulal Family Philanthropic Initiative (SFPI).

Source: [shikshalokam.org](https://shikshalokam.org/) (fetched 2026-05-10)

## Theory of change

> Leadership = Ability to Drive Continuous Micro-improvements

> anyone owning the responsibility to drive micro-improvement in the education ecosystem is a LEADER.

Operating cycle: **Sense → Make Sense → Learn → Improve**. The unit of change is the small, low-resource improvement, made by people already inside the system — not a programmatic intervention dropped from outside.

Source: [shikshalokam.org](https://shikshalokam.org/)
